DIBIA INC. — THE HIDDEN VILLAGE

A human compute organization, structured like a shinobi village.

Heterogeneous. Autonomous. Dynamic. Distributed. Decentralized. Diaspora-preserving. We read technology the way ninjutsu reads chakra — a discipline, a lineage, a set of seals passed hand to hand. Extraction is the enemy technique; giving is ours.

“You cannot transmute alone.”

THE UNIT OF WORK

Research moves in three-person cells.

Every mission is carried by a cell of three, guided by one to two Jonin- or Kage-level researchers. Small enough to move without noise, complete enough to carry a study end to end — the way a squad carries a mission.

Cells form across two tracks — academic and industry — and dissolve back into the village when the mission ships. Knowledge stays; hierarchy doesn’t.
