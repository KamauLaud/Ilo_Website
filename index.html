Who
I Am

I'm a PhD student in Computer Science at Virginia Tech, working in the 3D Interaction Group under Dr. Doug Bowman. My research sits at the intersection of human-computer interaction, augmented reality, and intelligent interfaces.

My goal is to build smart interaction techniques and intelligent user interfaces that adapt to a user's total context — their environment, task, time of day, and location. The vision: AR systems that infer intent and dynamically adjust without the user having to think about it.

One day, I hope to make open-world AR video games — where your body is the controller — a reality. When I'm not doing research, you'll find me gaming, working out, or planning the next trip.
